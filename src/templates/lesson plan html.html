Kodaly Songs

Cobbler Cobbler
5 min
Sol/Mi Song & Game
"Cobbler, cobbler, mend my shoe,
Get it done by half past two;
Stitch it up and stitch it down,
Then I'll give you half a crown."

Activities:

  • Children sit in circle, sing together
  • Keep beat by tapping shoes with hands/sticks
  • Hunt the Slipper: Customer in middle, others pass shoe secretly
  • Customer gets 3 tries to guess who has the shoe
